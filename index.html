Our Company

CONTEC de Mexico is an Electronic & Telecommunication company   located in Tlalnepantla de Baz,  EDOMEX with +25 years offering Repair & Refurbish services to the main MSOs  of the Telecommunications  industry  in Mexico.​In the  last 15  years CONTEC de Mexico  belonged to  the CONTEC LLC corporate giving Repair & Refurbish support  not only to  Mexican market  but  to  the  North  America market. ​​ ​​
This month CONTEC de Mexico change corporate from CONTEC LLC  to  CDS LLC ( Connectivity  and  Device Solutions) and got a  partnership with  Ensambladora de Matamoros  S de RL de CV (EDEMSA) which have  +37 years in the Electronic Manufacturing, Repair & refurbish  market, EDEMSA  has belonged  to  some  of the  most important  electronic  manufacturer  which was  General Instrument, MOTOROLA, ARRIS before  to belong  to CONTEC LLC and now belongs to CDS LLC.
